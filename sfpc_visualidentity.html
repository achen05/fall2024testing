School for Poetic Computation

Visual Identity Concept

Description

The School for Poetic Computation is an artist-run school in New York City that focuses on the intersection between art, code, hardware, and critical theory. It revolves around the idea of “poetic computation:” code that subverts traditional notions of programming as a form of cultural activism. This visual identity adopts strategies of poetic computation as a visual system, authentically representing poetic computation by being an act of it in and of itself.
